Introduction

I am a Mechanical Engineer and Master’s student in Applied Artificial Intelligence for Digital Production Management at Technische Hochschule Deggendorf, Germany.

With 3.5 years of experience in automotive product development, I have worked on BIW systems, CAD engineering, DFMEA, APQP, and manufacturing coordination for Citroën projects at Tata Consultancy Services.
